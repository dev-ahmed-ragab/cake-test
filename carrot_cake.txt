Carrot Cake Recipe

   Ingredients:
   - 2 cups flour
   - 1 cup sugar
   - 1/2 cup vegetable oil
   - 2 eggs
   - 1 tsp baking powder
   - 1 cup grated carrots
   - 1 tsp cinnamon

   Instructions:
   1. Mix oil and sugar.
   2. Add eggs and beat well.
   3. Add flour, baking powder, and cinnamon.
   4. Fold in grated carrots.
   5. Bake at 180Â°C for 40 minutes.
