Lemon Cake Recipe

   Ingredients:
   - 2 cups flour
   - 1 cup sugar
   - 1/2 cup butter
   - 2 eggs
   - 1 tsp baking powder
   - 1/4 cup lemon juice
   - Zest of 1 lemon

   Instructions:
   1. Mix butter and sugar until creamy.
   2. Add eggs, lemon juice, and lemon zest, then beat well.
   3. Add flour and baking powder, mix gently.
   4. Bake at 180Â°C for 35 minutes.
